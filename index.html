About Me

A private chef who truly cares about what you eat

Hi, I'm a Berlin-based private chef with a deep passion for fresh, seasonal, and nourishing food. I believe that eating well shouldn't be a luxury — it should be effortless and joyful. That's why I bring the full meal prep experience directly to your home.

From our first conversation to the last clean dish, I handle everything: menu planning tailored to your goals, sourcing the freshest local ingredients, cooking in your own kitchen, and leaving it spotless. You come home to a fridge full of beautiful, ready-to-eat meals.

Whether you're looking to eat healthier, save time, or simply enjoy restaurant-quality food at home — I'm here to make it happen, every single week.

I speak: 🇬🇧 English 🇩🇪 Deutsch 🇷🇺 Русский
The Process

How it works — simple as that

Four easy steps from first contact to a fridge full of delicious, healthy meals.

01
💬

We talk about your needs & goals

A relaxed consultation to understand your dietary preferences, health goals, household size, and schedule. No commitment needed.

02
📋

I plan your weekly menu

A fully personalized menu crafted around your tastes, nutritional needs, and the best seasonal ingredients available that week.

03
🛒

I shop for fresh ingredients

I source the freshest, highest-quality ingredients — from local markets and trusted suppliers — so you never have to worry about shopping.

04
✨

I cook & clean up at your home

I arrive, cook everything in your kitchen, label and store your meals — then leave it cleaner than I found it. You just enjoy the food.
